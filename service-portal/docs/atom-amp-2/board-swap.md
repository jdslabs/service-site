# Main Board Replacement

Replace the main board in your Atom Amp 2.

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Time:** 10 minutes  
**Tools:** Phillips head screwdriver, hex key (for setscrew)

---

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-2/board-swap-1.JPG" alt="Disconnect power" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">1</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Disconnect Power</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Unplug the power cable from your Atom Amp 2</li>
      <li>Disconnect any audio cables (RCA, headphones)</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="repair-warning">
      Always disconnect power before opening the enclosure to avoid electrical shock or damage.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-2/board-swap-2.JPG" alt="Remove side panel screws" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">2</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Side Panel Screws</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Locate the 4 Phillips head screws on the side panels at the rear of the device</li>
      <li>Remove all 4 screws</li>
      <li>Set the screws aside in a safe place to avoid losing them</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-2/board-swap-3.JPG" alt="Remove enclosure panels" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">3</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Enclosure Panels</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Separate the rear panel from the enclosure</li>
      <li>Slide the top panel rearwards to remove it</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="repair-info">
      Work on a clean, static-free surface to protect the electronics.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-2/board-swap-4.JPG" alt="Remove the knob" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">4</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove the Knob</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Loosen the setscrew on the knob</li>
      <li>Remove the knob from the potentiometer shaft</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-2/board-swap-5.JPG" alt="Remove circuit board screws" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">5</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Circuit Board Screws</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Loosen and remove the 4 Phillips head screws securing the circuit board</li>
      <li>Carefully lift the board from the enclosure</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media"></div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">6</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Reassembly</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Follow Steps 1-5 in reverse order to reassemble your Atom Amp 2 with the new board</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
