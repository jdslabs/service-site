# Main Board Replacement

Replace the main board in your Atom Amp+.

**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Time:** 10 minutes  
**Tools:** Torx screwdriver

---

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-1.JPG" alt="Disconnect power" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">1</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Disconnect Power</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Unplug the power cable from your Atom Amp+</li>
      <li>Disconnect any audio cables (RCA, headphones)</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="repair-warning">
      Always disconnect power before opening the enclosure to avoid electrical shock or damage.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-2.JPG" alt="Remove the knob" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">2</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove the Knob</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Pinch the volume knob and remove it from the potentiometer shaft</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-3.JPG" alt="Remove bottom screws" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">3</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Bottom Screws</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Remove all four feet from the bottom of the device</li>
      <li>Remove the four Torx head screws</li>
      <li>Set the screws aside in a safe place to avoid losing them</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-4.JPG" alt="Remove top panel" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">4</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Top Panel</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Tip the device over to remove the top panel</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="repair-info">
      Work on a clean, static-free surface to protect the electronics.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-5.JPG" alt="Detach rear panel" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">5</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Detach Rear Panel</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Lightly bend the top of the rear case inwards to detach the adhesive of the rear panel</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media">
    <img src="/img/atom-amp-plus/board-swap-6.JPG" alt="Remove main board" />
  </div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">6</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Remove Main Board</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Slide the main board out of the void left by the removed rear panel</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="repair-step">
  <div class="repair-step-media"></div>
  <div class="repair-step-content">
    <div class="repair-step-header">
      <span class="repair-step-number">7</span>
      <h3 class="repair-step-title">Reassembly</h3>
    </div>
    <ul>
      <li>Follow Steps 1-6 in reverse order to reassemble your Atom Amp+ with the new board</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
